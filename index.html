How to Use Watermark-It

1

Upload Images

Drag and drop your property images, upload files/ZIP archives, or add images via URL. Supports JPG, PNG, and WebP formats. Note: HEIC files may experience processing issues - convert to JPG for best results.

2

Configure Watermark

Choose from our professional Fort Lowell Realty watermark gallery or upload your own custom watermark. Adjust position, opacity, and size to perfectly brand your property images.

3

Preview & Process

Generate a live preview to see exactly how your watermarked images will look. Use the enlarge button for fullscreen preview with keyboard/swipe navigation. Process all images and download as a ZIP archive.

Pro Tips

  • Use high-resolution images for best watermark quality
  • Lower opacity for subtle branding, higher for protection
  • Bottom-right position is ideal for property photos
  • Process multiple images at once for efficiency
